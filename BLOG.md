# Automate Zcash Operations with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-zCash, a powerful community node that brings Zcash cryptocurrency operations directly into your n8n workflows!

## The Challenge

Managing Zcash wallets, handling shielded transactions, and monitoring mining operations typically requires manual intervention or custom scripting. For businesses and developers working with privacy-focused cryptocurrency solutions, this creates friction in operational workflows and limits automation possibilities.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-zCash

Developed by Velocity BPA, this community node bridges the gap between Zcash's privacy-preserving technology and n8n's powerful workflow automation platform. Now you can seamlessly integrate Zcash operations into your existing automation workflows without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features

**Wallet Management**: Create, monitor, and manage Zcash wallets directly from your n8n workflows. Track balances, generate addresses, and handle multiple wallets with ease.

**Shielded Operations**: Leverage Zcash's signature privacy features by automating shielded transactions. Move funds between transparent and shielded addresses, ensuring privacy when you need it most.

**Mining Integration**: Monitor mining operations, track performance metrics, and automate responses to mining events—all within your n8n automation ecosystem.

## Getting Started

Installing n8n-nodes-zCash is straightforward. Simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-zCash


After installation, restart your n8n instance, and you'll find the Zcash node available in your node palette, ready to be dropped into any workflow.
